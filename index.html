Objection Responses
3 objections sourced from public category complaints and your site's positioning gaps
Q"We already have Teams and Zoom."
Teams and Zoom are for scheduled meetings. This is for blocked work between meetings — context gaps that create back-and-forth threads and day-long delays. They're not substitutes; you'd use both.
Q"This will be another tool nobody uses."
Adoption fails when tools ask teams to change everything at once. This works because you start with one moment: blocked handoffs. One workflow, one page, one short script — adoption comes from a specific problem, not a mandate.
Q"Video takes longer than just typing it."
Agreed — for simple questions. But when context is ambiguous, typed messages create 6–8 rounds of back-and-forth. A 2-minute walkthrough replaces the entire thread. The right tool for the right moment — not video-for-everything.
